Our Story

A place to unwind
and reconnect

Escape the city and enjoy the simple beauty of nature at Camp Bughaw. Whether you're looking to relax, spend time with family and friends, or make lasting memories — this is the perfect place to slow down.

We built Bughaw around the things that matter out here: the sound of the river, the warmth of a bonfire, food shared at a long wooden table, and the kind of stillness you can only find under an open sky in Tanay.

Everything is made by hand and made to last — woven bamboo, thatched nipa, lanterns strung between the posts. Come as guests. Leave with stories.
