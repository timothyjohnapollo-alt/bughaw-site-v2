Our Story

A place to unwind
and reconnect

Camp Bughaw is a pet-friendly camping and glamping destination nestled along the tranquil Agos River in Barangay Daraitan, Tanay, Rizal. Established to provide an accessible escape into nature, the camp was created for individuals, families, and groups seeking a peaceful outdoor experience.

The name "Bughaw," the Filipino word for blue, was inspired by the Agos River and the serene beauty of its natural surroundings. It reflects the calm, refreshing atmosphere that defines the Camp Bughaw experience.
